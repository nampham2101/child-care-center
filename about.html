About Sunny Days

A community-rooted center dedicated to nurturing curious, confident kids.

Our Mission

Sunny Days Child Care Center exists to give every child a safe, joyful, and stimulating place to grow — and to give parents peace of mind while they're at work or school. We partner with families to support each child's social, emotional, and academic development from infancy through pre-K.

Our Philosophy

Play is how children learn

We build our days around hands-on, play-based activities rather than worksheets — because that's how young children absorb language, math, and social skills best.

Every child is unique

Teachers get to know each child's interests and pace, and adapt activities so everyone feels challenged and supported.

Families are partners

We share daily updates and check in regularly so you always know how your child's day went.
