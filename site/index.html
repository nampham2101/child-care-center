Why families choose Sunny Days

Caring, qualified staff

Our teachers are trained in early childhood education and CPR/first aid, and stay with your child's group all year.

Play-based learning

Children build early literacy, motor, and social skills through guided play, songs, and hands-on activities.

Safe, secure campus

Keypad entry, sign-in/sign-out logs, and a fenced outdoor play area keep your child safe throughout the day.

Programs for every age

Infants (6 weeks – 12 months)

Individualized care with a low teacher-to-child ratio.

Toddlers (1 – 2 years)

Exploration-focused days that build independence and language.

Preschool & Pre-K (3 – 5 years)

School-readiness curriculum covering letters, numbers, and social skills.
