Our Programs

Age-appropriate classrooms and curriculum from 6 weeks through pre-K.

[Photo: Infant Room]

Infants — 6 weeks to 12 months

Our infant room offers individualized daily schedules for feeding, napping, and play, with a low teacher-to-child ratio so every baby gets attentive, loving care. Teachers track daily routines and share updates with families each day.

[Photo: Toddler Room]

Toddlers — 1 to 2 years

Toddlers explore their growing independence through sensory play, music, and simple group activities that build language and motor skills, all in a safe and supervised environment.

[Photo: Preschool Room]

Preschool — 3 to 4 years

Our preschool curriculum introduces letters, numbers, and early science concepts through hands-on projects, storytime, and guided play that builds a love of learning.

[Photo: Pre-K Room]

Pre-K — 4 to 5 years

Our pre-K program focuses on kindergarten readiness: early reading and writing, math foundations, and social-emotional skills like sharing, patience, and following routines.
